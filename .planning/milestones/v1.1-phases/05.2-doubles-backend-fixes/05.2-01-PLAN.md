Fix: after parsing resultJson, if `resultJson?.winner` is absent/null, derive the winning slot direction directly from the `winnerId` parameter by comparing it against `updatedMatch.pair1Id` / `pair2Id` (for doubles) or `player1Id` / `player2Id` (for singles).
