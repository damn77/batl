## What Was Built

### Task 1: Prisma Schema Changes and Migration

Two targeted changes to `backend/prisma/schema.prisma`:

1. **Match.player1Id made nullable** (`String` → `String?`): Allows future-round placeholder matches to be created without player IDs. The match record exists in the bracket structure before a winner is determined and promoted into that slot. The corresponding relation was also updated from `PlayerProfile` to `PlayerProfile?`.

2. **Tournament.registrationClosed added** (`Boolean @default(false)`): Enables organizers to explicitly lock registration before generating the draw. The `generateBracket()` service (Plan 02) will check this flag as a prerequisite guard.
