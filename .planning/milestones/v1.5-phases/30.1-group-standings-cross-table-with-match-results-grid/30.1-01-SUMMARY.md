## Accomplishments

- CrossTable.jsx component with useMemo matchLookup for O(1) cell rendering across N entities
- Score rendering handles both BIG_TIEBREAK/single-set (game score e.g., "6-4") and multi-set (set count e.g., "2-1") from row player's perspective
- Initials computation with doubles pair support ("AL/BM") and numeric collision suffix ("AL", "AL2")
- All five cell states implemented: diagonal (grey), win (green bold), loss (muted), unplayed (en-dash, muted), cross-highlighted (blue bg)
- Full keyboard accessibility: tabIndex=0, Enter/Space keydown, aria-label with score and win/loss state
